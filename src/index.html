FAQ

DIETARY REQUIREMENTS BOOKINGS GENERAL
01
Do you cater to GF?
We always have a GF pasta available, made on site daily and ready to be subbed in with any of our GF pasta sauces. We also have GF small plates on the menu!
02
Do you cater to Vegans?
Yes! As our regular pasta is made using eggs, we also make a fresh vegan pasta in house daily which is available on request. There are also a number of vegan friendly small plates on the menu.

For any booking amendments or queries regarding your booking, please emaill eat@sprezzatura.ie - we cannot guarantee all requests will be accommodated.
02
How do I cancel my booking?
To cancel your booking follow the link included in your Resmio confirmation email.

We will always try to accommodate walk-ins where possible!
